EVENTS 2010-2013

Mahendi

A 6-month certified course on Mehendi was organized wherein 20 females were divided into 2 batches and were trained for the same. Today due to its success 20 females lead their families by converting this of art into a source of income.

Mansi Seva Sakhi Mandal with the support of Rotary Club organized a 3-month training program on "Prodh Shikshan" wherein they distributed slates and all other stationery with the aim to motivate students to study.

On the occasion of 15th August (Independence Day) we conducted drawing and painting competition with the support of Anand Niketan School. A camp of 30 students participated. The main aim behind conduction this was the growth and motivation of the children.

On the occasion of Christmas Eve, we distributed compass boxes, bags, etc. to kids. The aim behind this was to promote education in the slums.

Training Programme

Our Organization in tie-up with Mahatma Gandhi Labor Institute from the last 3 years has been conducting training for around 400 females each year. In Kamdar Kalyan Board around 50females have given paper and construction work and around 70 females used to work in Rasoda Kamdar Board.

We on a regular yearly basis distribute books to the children of the women that are involved with us. The main aim behind this is to motivate the kids to study and also to create awareness for the parents to send their kids to school.

EVENTS 2014-2019

Mansi started working on helping of paying fees of schools to the women that are with the organization.

Distributing books have been one of the activities that Mansi does every year to promote education for the underprivileged. Free books are distributed to each and every member of the staff and everyone who is a part of Mansi Seva Sakhi Mandal.

Mahendi

Mansi conducted picnic for the senior citizens that live in the chalis. They observed that the old aged groups basically sit the whole day in their homes and don't get any chance to have any trip or to explore new places. So they planned on conducting a picnic for them. .

Mansi conducts general awareness meetings for providing the slums basic information and awareness on general issues such as water conservation, ill-effects of tobacco/alcohol, general issues that women face, etc. For women to show up in huge numbers they distribute steel dabas to all the women.

Mahendi

In order to keep the women working under Mansi to stay motivated they organized a picnic for all the home managers under Mansi Livelihood Services.

Mansi by using the reach of its contacts managed to conduct a training secession at the Taj Hotels in Baroda. It was a full day event and helped most of the women for the information and tips from a completely different perspective. It was conducted for the home managers involved with Mansi Livelihood Services.

Mahendi

Mansi started communicating with the clients to give the home manager senior citizen women to provide them with some basic help (‘Arthik-Sahai’). It helps them to get some basic help from the clients for the work the hard work Home Managers do for them.

Free full body check-ups are conducted every year to check the health of all the staff members as well as for all the women involved with Mansi Livelihood and Mansi Seva Sakhi Mandal and also in the slums. .
