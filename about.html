About US

Give Us hand to make world better

Mansi Organization in its initial period was started with only 3 female members as staff. It still has the total limited staff of 6 members. It started first with gaining the trust of the people of the slums to invest in the savings program. This was done by making them know that the founder, as well as the staff members, belong to the exact same surrounding area and the NGO is working for their cause i.e., to uplift them from poverty.

The main aim to start the savings scheme was to remove the system of the slum people taking money from the moneylenders. Mansi identified that the main threat faced by the slums for the loan were moneylenders. Moneylenders, though being a quick source for cash, charged skyrocketed high interests for the loans, due to this the poor have to give most of their monthly earnings for repaying the loans along with the interests.

In the span of around 10 years the organization has earned tremendous success in the all-round development of slum dwellers as well has changed their lives.

A place where girls drop out of schools from 7 th Grade just because she no longer wants to go and her parents think that it’s okay! If she drops out, Place where housewives don’t know how to sign on legal papers, a place so deeply stuck in the same old cycle of poverty that getting of it seems impossible. This is the ugly picture of the backstory of the place we call our society. Is this what we want for our city of Ahmedabad?"

Mansi Seva Sakhi Mandal is an initiative of Mansi Livelihood Services established in August 2009. Mansi Livelihood Services is a business entity which acts as a mediator by mentoring the socially and economically underprivileged women for household chores and giving them job opportunities in highly elite homes.

Mansi Seva Sakhi Mandal is a Non-Profit Organization which provides a support system for the slums in Ahmedabad. It runs a bank type system wherein the people from the slums can invest and save a little of their earnings every month and on the time of their need Mansi Seva Sakhi Mandal provides them with loans on lower interest rates as well as on EMI.
