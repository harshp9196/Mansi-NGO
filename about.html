About US

Give Us hand to make world better

Mansi consists of three different-different entities under one roof. Mansi Livelihood Services that runs the Home Assistant Program which is a business organization, Mansi Bachat Mandal runs Savings and Credit Scheme which is the Self Help Group(SHG) and then The Mansi Charitable Trust that conducts workshops, awareness programs, adult education (‘Prodh Sikshan’) and other developmental activities for the underprivileged..

Mansi Organization in its initial period was started with only 3 female members as staff. It still has the total limited staff of 6 members. It started first with gaining the trust of the people of the slums. This was done by visiting them in their homes and understanding them. The founder Kokilaben herself, as well as the staff members, visited the slums even today to understand the grass-root problems of the slums and how can they work on improving the conditions..

MANSI LIVELIHOOD SERVICES

Mansi Livelihood services have Home Assistant Program that trains the underprivileged women to be self-independent for working and earning a living for themselves. It is a business entity that acts as a mediator between these women and the clients that want to render services such as household chores, taking care of an infant, etc.

There are fixed hours for a woman to work and is paid according to the number of hours and the amount of work that the client makes her do every day. A woman working in Mansi Livelihood Services earns more than a normal ‘Kaamwali Bai’ and also the clients are bound to provide some perks to the women working for them. Home Assistant Program is a registered business entity and Mansi earns profit from it by acting as a mediator. But it also has helped women from the slums to uplift themselves by providing for their families..

MANSI BACHAT MANDAL

Mansi Bachat Mandal is a Self Help Group (SGH) it is a savings scheme for helping the people from slums to save some of their monthly earnings and understand the importance of saving instead of spending all out. The main aim to start the savings scheme was to remove the system of the slum people taking money from the moneylenders. Mansi identified that the main threat faced by the slums for the loan were moneylenders. Moneylenders, though being a quick source for cash, charged skyrocketed high interests for the loans, due to this the poor have to give most of their monthly earnings for repaying the loans along with the interests..

In the span of around 10 years the organization has earned tremendous success in the all-round development of slum dwellers as well has changed their lives..

MANSI CHARITABLE TRUST

Mansi Charitable Trust works only and only for the development of the slums. It conducts workshops for general awareness, picnics, educational programs, etc. for women. Additionally, it also provides for the fees of girls whose families are not capable of educating them and also other support to the underprivileged society. It works in the best interests of the people for the slums and finds ways to uplift them in the best way they could with the resources that they have.
